Design Notes
Bottom Sheet Pattern Decision cards appear as bottom sheet overlays that slide up from the cart screen. Maximizes mobile screen real estate while keeping context visible.
Progress Indicator 4 dots show step progression (1✓, 2✓, 3●, 4○). Current step shown in header. Visual confirmation of workflow progress.
Handle & Swipe Visible handle bar at top suggests swipe-to-dismiss pattern (standard iOS). Full-height content is scrollable if needed.
Touch-Friendly Buttons Large button targets (48px min height) for mobile. Primary action (Add to RO) is green; secondary (Decline) is outlined.
Card Details Service name, price, and acceptance rate shown in pill-shaped metadata box. Historical context ("Jan 15, 2026", "5mm → 3mm") builds advisor confidence.
Footer Message Tells advisor how many decisions remain ("T1 has 1 more decision"). Creates urgency and sense of completion.
