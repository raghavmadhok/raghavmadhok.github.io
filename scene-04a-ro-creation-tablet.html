Design Notes
T1 Decision Cards Pattern Four decision cards guide advisors through RO creation: Tag detection (orange), damage photos (blue), deferred service upsell (amber), and final summary (green). Each card shows actionable buttons and relevant context.
Card States Completed: green border + dimmed + checkmark prefix. Active: full color + prominent shadow + buttons visible. Pending: gray border + dimmed until reached.
Left Panel: Cart UI (55%) Tekion Cart pattern with vehicle header, job items with opcode badges, and pricing. Totals update as cards are actioned.
Right Panel: T1 Chat (45%) Stacked decision cards with icons/emojis, titles, descriptions, and buttons. Completed cards collapse with green check. Active card (deferred service #3) is fully expanded.
Deferred Service Upsell Shows historical context ("Jan 15, 2026"), wear data ("5mm → 3mm"), and conversion rate ("73%") to help advisor make informed decision. Primary CTA is to add the service.
Voice Integration Pulsing mic button + "Listening..." text allows advisor to speak commands. T1 processes voice and surfaces next decision card automatically.
